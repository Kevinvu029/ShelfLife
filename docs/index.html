About this app

Stop throwing money in the trash. ShelfLife is a smart pantry manager that tracks the expiration dates of everything in your fridge, freezer, and cupboards — so you always know what to use first.

Scan any product barcode and ShelfLife instantly identifies the item, pulls its image, and records it to your personal pantry. Set the expiry date and you're done. The app's color-coded dashboard shows you what's fresh (green), expiring soon (yellow), or already past date (red) at a glance.

Key Features:

  • 📷 Barcode Scanner — Point your camera and ShelfLife looks up the product automatically via Open Food Facts and our custom database.
  • ⏱️ Expiry Tracking — Color-coded indicators: red = expired, yellow = within 7 days, green = safe.
  • 🗄️ Pantry List — All your food in one scrollable, sortable view.
  • 💰 Price Tracking — Log what you paid to see how much money you're saving.
  • 🔐 Secure Cloud Sync — Sign in with email. Data syncs securely across all your devices via Firebase.
  • 🌍 Community — See how your household stacks up against other ShelfLife users on the leaderboard.

The average American family throws away $1,500 worth of food per year. ShelfLife gives you the visibility to change that habit — one scan at a time.

Built with Flutter · Firebase · Open Food Facts · Google Cloud Run
