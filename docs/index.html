Everything You Need

ShelfLife makes pantry management effortless, from scan to reminder.

📷

Barcode Scanning

Point your camera at any product barcode and ShelfLife instantly looks up the item's name and image — no typing required.

⏱️

Expiry Tracking

tells you at a glance what's fresh, expiring soon, or already past its date.

🗄️

Pantry List

Browse everything in your fridge and cupboards in one organized view, sorted and ready to act on.

💰

Price Tracking

Log what you paid for each item so you can see exactly how much food — and money — you're saving.

🔍

Product Lookup

Powered by Open Food Facts and a custom product database so coverage is broad and constantly growing.

🔐

Secure Accounts

Sign in with Email. Your pantry data syncs securely to the cloud via Firebase.

Three Simple Steps

Adding items to your pantry takes seconds.

01

Scan or Search

Use your phone's camera to scan a barcode, or search by name. Product info is pulled in automatically.

02

Set the Date

Confirm the expiration date with the date picker and save the item to your pantry in one tap.

03

Stay Ahead

ShelfLife flags items expiring within 7 days so you can use them before it's too late.
